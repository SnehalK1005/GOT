The different families in Game of Thrones have a major impact on the plot, especially the great houses.

These families lead a designated region of Westeros, giving them power above other lords and a larger role in the kingdom's politics.

HOUSE OF STARK

Based in Winterfell, House Stark is known for its strong sense of honor and duty

HOUSE OF LANNISTER

Based in Casterly Rock, House Lannister is one of the wealthiest and most powerful houses in Westeros

HOUSE OF TARGARYEN

Once the ruling family of Westeros, House Targaryen was overthrown in Robert’s Rebellion, which began when Prince Rhaegar Targaryen abducted Lyanna Stark

HOUSE OF BARATHEON

Based in Storm’s End, House Baratheon rose to power when Robert Baratheon overthrew the Targaryen dynasty in Robert’s Rebellion

HOUSE OF GREYJOY

Based in the Iron Islands, House Greyjoy is a seafaring family known for their piracy and raiding

HOUSE OF TYRELL

Based in Highgarden, House Tyrell is known for their wealth and political influence

HOUSE OF ARRYN

Based in the Eyrie, House Arryn is known for their isolation and defense

HOUSE OF MARTELL

Based in Dorne, House Martell is known for their fierce independence and unique culture

HOUSE OF TULLY

Based of Riverrun, House Tully ruled over the Riverlands from the castle and the head of the house is the Lord of Riverrun.

HOUSE OF MORMONT

Based on Bear Island, House Moremont is known for the possession of Valyrian sword Longclaw, which later was given to Jon Snow.
